Summary
This service manages the lifecycle of school entities and their associated profiles within the EduTrack system. It handles core CRUD operations for schools, including creation, updates, and deletion, while enforcing access control for headmasters. The service also manages school profiles by retrieving, adding, and updating them in relation to specific schools. Additionally, it processes school schedules and term configurations to support the e-journal's temporal structure. All data transformations between entities and DTOs are centralized within this class to maintain consistency. This service acts as the primary business logic layer for school-related operations in the application.
