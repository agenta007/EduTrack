Summary
The StudentService class manages the lifecycle of student enrollments within the EduTrack system, specifically handling the processes of adding and removing students from schools. It provides methods to retrieve a list of available students who are not currently enrolled and to enroll a user by linking them to the headmaster's school. The service also supports expelling students by clearing their school association after verifying that the requesting headmaster has authority over that specific student. Internal helper methods ensure that the authenticated user is a valid headmaster and that they are associated with a school before performing any enrollment or expulsion actions. This service acts as the business logic layer for student-school relationships, enforcing role-based access control and data integrity.
