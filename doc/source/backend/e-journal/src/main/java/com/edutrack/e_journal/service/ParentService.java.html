Summary
ParentService manages the lifecycle of parent accounts and their associations with students within the EduTrack system. It provides methods to retrieve parents linked to a specific school, fetch all available parent users, and handle the linking or unlinking of students to parents. The service enforces security by verifying that the authenticated headmaster has authority over the relevant school and students before performing any modifications. It also supports updating parent profile details such as name and email. By grouping students under their respective parents, it facilitates efficient data retrieval for the school administration interface.
